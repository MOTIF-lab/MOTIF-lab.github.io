The Mobility Optimized by Traditional Ideas and Frontiers (MOTIF) Lab at the University of South Florida, led by Prof. Hao Zhou, conducts cutting-edge research at the intersection of self-driving technologies, electric vehicles, and generative AI. The lab vision is to cultivate a synergy between classical transportation theories and emerging AI technologies to advance and transform transportation mobility.
